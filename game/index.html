Catch the Falling Stars

Use the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys to move the basket and catch the falling stars. If a star falls past the basket, you lose a life. Lose all your lives and the game ends.

Golden stars are worth +3 points, fall faster, and never cost you a life if missed!

Watch out for dark bombs - catching one costs you a life!

From level 2, gusts of wind can push the stars and your basket. From level 3, icy stars will slow your basket down if caught. From level 4, watch out for lightning strikes!
